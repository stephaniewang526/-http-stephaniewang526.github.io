I am a senior majoring in Information Science and minoring in Business at Cornell University. I am also working as the assistant webmaster in Cornell College of Human Ecology and a software developer intern at IBM's Cloud Computing team.

I enjoy peer-to-peer learning and knowledge sharing. I am a TA for CS4300, Language and Information. I TAed INFO1300, Introduction to Web Design and Programming, and CS2800, Discrete Structures, in the past.

I'm a passionate web developer and front-end engineer. In addition to managing the Cornell Human Ecology website, I also work with my own clients to help them develop aesthetic and viable websites (check out my work below). I'm working on a thesis on Web Interface Design for Chinese Characters. This past summer, I worked as a developer on the UX/UI team at MasterCard. I exercised agile software developement practice on different projects and went through many iterations of prototyping using both Axure and native code (HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript). The previous summer, I worked as a technology associate at the renown Upstate New York venture accelerator, StartFast. I helped develop websites for several startup companies and focused mainly on back-end development using PHP.

I am very passionate about entrepreneurship and leadership. I am the chief curator in charge of Ithaca area at Upstate Venture Connect. I also served as the project manager coordinator of Cornell Data Science Club and the student advisor for Dean Boor of College of Agriculture and Life Sciences (CALS) for over a year.
